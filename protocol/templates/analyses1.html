The PIXL (Planetary Instrument for X-ray Lithochemistry)
onboard the Mars-2020 rover is a microfocus XRF (fluorescence) instrument.
It measures elemental chemistry at sub-millimeter scales
by focusing an X-ray beam to a tiny spot on the target rock or soil and analyzing the induced X-ray fluorescence.
